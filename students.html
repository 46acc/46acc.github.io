Student Prize

The CMSA Anne Penfold Street Student Prizeis awarded to the student who is judged to have given the best presentation at the ACC. If you answer "Yes" to the "Are you a student?" question during registration, you will automatically be considered for the student prize. If you are a student and you do not wish to be considered, or if you have any other questions about eligibility for the award, please email the organisers (46acc@uq.edu.au).
