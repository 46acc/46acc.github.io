The CMSA student prize is awarded to the student who is judged to have given the best presentation at the ACCMCC. If you answer "Yes" to the "Are you a student?" question during registration, you will automatically be considered for the student prize. If you are a student and you do not wish to be considered, or if you have any other questions about eligibility for the award, please email the organiser (46ACC@uq.edu.au).
