Off-campus Accommodation

There are accommodation options to suit all budgets available within the Brisbane CBD, Southbank, and Toowong. These areas have convenient public transportation options (bus and City Cat ferry). Some suggested options:

  • Inn on the Park
  • Jephson Hotel
  • Sofitel Brisbane Central
  • Ibis Styles Brisbane
  • Rydges South Bank
  • Mantra South Bank Brisbane
  • Other options are available via booking webbsites such as booking.comk and Airbnb.
