Registration

Registration

The registration fees (in Australian dollars) are as follows.

Type Early Bird Standard
Standard $440 $520
CMSA life member $405 $485
Unwaged $240 $280

Which registration type?

The Unwaged registration is for students, retirees and the unemployed. If you are unsure of your eligibility, please contact the organisers (46acc@uq.edu.au). Further information for students can be found here.

There is a AUD$35 reduction in the price of registration for Life Members of the CMSA (Combinatorial Mathematics Society of Australasia) who are not unwaged. (Unwaged Life Members of the CMSA should choose the Unwaged registration type, which is already heavily discounted.)

The early bird deadline is 1 November 2024.

CMSA membership for one year

All registrants for 46ACC receive free membership of the CMSA for 2025. On the registration form, CMSA life members should select “Yes” when asked about free membership of the CMSA for 2024.

Extras

One conference dinner ticket is included with registration. When you are paying the registration fee (via the link given above), you will also be able to order:

  • extra tickets to the conference dinner
  • tickets to the excursion

Details about the conference dinner and excursion can be found here

Questions?

If you have questions or expereince any problems with registration, please contact the organisers at 46acc@uq.edu.au.
