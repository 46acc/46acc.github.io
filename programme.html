Excursion

Our excursion will be on Wednesday 4 December to Lone Pine Koala Sanctuary. The coach will leave UQ St. Lucia Campus, Chancellors Place at 11:00am and take us directly to Lone Pine. You may wish to bring your own lunch to the excursion, or lunch may be purhcased at one of the two cafes at Lone Pine. While out on the excursion, we highly recommend that you take the opportunity to feed the kangaroos and attend at least one of these free shows: Raptor show (1pm), Sheep Dog show (1:30pm), Dingo talk (2:45pm) and Wild Lorikeet feeding (3:45pm). The return journey coach will pick up from Lone Pine at 5:00pm and return to UQ St. Lucia Campus, Chancellors Place.
